What we do

We are a nonprofit that empowers loval community-led nonprofits through tools, training, and suport so then can make our world a better place.

For Nonprofits:

Support, training, and funding.

Nonprofit organizations, big or small, can get the exposure, support, encouragement, and tools they need to become more effective.

For Donors:

Safe, easy donations.

Donors everywhere can give confidently and securely to vetted, locally-driven organizations around the world.

For Companies:

Corporate giving, simplified.

Companies can do more good with their charitable donations through employee engagement, gift cards and more.

For nonprofits:

Why Join GlobalGiving?

You need a partner, not just a platform. Join organizations like yours from 170 countries and get training and support tailored to you, access extra funding through our thematic matching campaigns, and improve your fundraising and your impact with our powerful suite of tools.

Join the 200+ companies doing good around the world.

Is your company looking for ways to expand your philanthropic footprint? We have the tools and programs you need to enhance your corporate giving strategy, empower your stakeholders, and support local causes around the world.

Our approach to disaster relief and recovery

When a disaster happens, we can quickly send funds to the local organizations that are best suited to drive relief and recovery. Our vetted, locally-driven partner organizations are still hard at work driving recovery even after the news coverage has stopped. Local partners know what's best for their communities, especially in the face of disaster.

Sign up for the GlobalGiving Newsletter

GlobalGiving makes it easy and safe for you to give to local projects anywhere in the world, while providing nonprofits with the tools, training, and support they need to become more effective.
